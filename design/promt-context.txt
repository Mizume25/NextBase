NextBase is a financial advisory web application that connects 
clients with their financial advisors.

The platform has two types of users:

1. Customer (authenticated user) — a client who can:
   - Register and log in to the platform
   - View and update their own profile
   - Request financial advisory services:
     * Free Consultation
     * Financial Advice
     * Administrative Advice
   - View their generated records (expedients)
   - View their tickets, documents and invoices
   - All data is private and only visible to the customer

2. Manager (service role) — a financial advisor who can:
   - Manage their assigned customers (full CRUD)
   - Create and manage records for each customer
   - Generate tickets (appointments or resolutions)
   - Upload documents
   - Generate invoices
   - View audit logs

Core entities:
- Customers: clients managed by a user
- Records: financial expedients with type and status
- Tickets: appointments or resolutions linked to a record
- Documents: files uploaded by the manager
- Invoices: billing linked to a record and customer

Tech stack:
- Next.js 15 (App Router, Server Actions)
- TypeScript
- Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, RLS)
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui components

The app is dark themed, professional and modern, 
inspired by fintech SaaS platforms.
Primary color is blue (#3B82F6) on a deep navy 
background (#0A0F1E).
